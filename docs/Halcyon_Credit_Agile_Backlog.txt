Halcyon Credit — Agile Backlog & Sprint Plan
Agentic Underwriting Copilot  ·  PRD v2.0  ·  Sprint 0 → Sprint 4

1. Project Overview
Halcyon Credit is a digital consumer lender serving applicants with thin or non-traditional credit histories. The PRD specifies a human-in-the-loop Agentic Underwriting Copilot that:
Assembles application facts from upstream systems
Predicts repayment risk via a trained ML model
Evaluates compliance against the applicable loan-scheme policy using a RAG engine
Verifies documents and regulatory signals
Audits decisions for fairness
Produces an evidence-backed Approve / Decline / Refer recommendation
The system recommends, it does not decide. Final authority remains with a licensed human underwriter.

2. Functional Scope
In scope (PRD §5):
Probability-of-default prediction (supervised ML on structured data)
Multi-scheme policy evaluation: Personal, Education, Vehicle, Business, First-Time Borrower, Low-Income
RAG policy engine grounding every recommendation in retrieved clauses
Policy version management; scheme-aware recommendations
Fairness monitoring across protected & proxy demographic segments
Evidence-backed recommendation (Approve / Decline / Refer)
Human-in-the-loop workflow with defined escalation triggers
Immutable audit logging (inputs, outputs, policy checks, fairness, recommendation)
Retrieval quality monitoring (context precision, recall, faithfulness)
Underwriter productivity & cost-per-application monitoring
Out of scope (PRD §5):
Autonomous approve/decline
Live bureau / KYC integrations (mocked in v1)
Production-grade deployment / regulatory licensing
Applicant-facing portal
Fraud detection beyond dataset signals
Multi-jurisdiction legal compliance beyond simulated corpus
LGD / EAD modelling (future V2)
